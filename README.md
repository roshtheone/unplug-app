## The Problem

Brain rot is not a meme — it's a measurable outcome.

The average person spends over **7 hours a day on their phone** — more time than they sleep. Social media platforms are engineered to exploit boredom, anxiety, and loneliness. Every scroll is a response to something missing.

Every existing app that claims to fix this does the same thing: it blocks other apps, shows a usage timer, and calls it done.

**That is not a solution. It's a label on a wound.**

---

## What UnPlug Does Differently

Most screen-time apps treat the symptom. UnPlug identifies the **root trigger** behind every phone reach — and replaces it with something real.

| Trigger | UnPlug Response |
|---|---|
| 😰 Anxiety | Structured breathing protocol + wellness remedy |
| 😔 Loneliness | Stranger dare or group activity invite |

### 🤖 Dora — AI Mentor

Most AI assistants give you the same answer regardless of who you are. Dora doesn't.

Dora is UnPlug's premium AI mentor — built on the understanding that a 19-year-old CS student in Charlotte needs completely different guidance than a 34-year-old marketing manager in Austin. Generic advice is noise. Dora gives signal.

**How Dora works:**
- During onboarding, you tell Dora your profile — your field, your goal, your city, and your timeline

**What makes Dora different from ChatGPT or any other AI:**
ChatGPT knows nothing about you unless you tell it every time. Dora is woven into your UnPlug profile. She sees your behavior patterns, your check-in history, your nutrition data, and your location context — and she gives you guidance that actually fits your life.

> Dora is a **premium feature** — because real mentorship has always been the most valuable and least accessible resource for young people building their futures. UnPlug changes that.

## Why This Matters

Screen addiction is a behavioral health problem. The health industry has poured billions into physical wearables — heart rate monitors, glucose trackers, sleep rings. These are extraordinary tools.

**But no one is building the behavioral layer.**

No device tells you *why* you reached for your phone at 2am. No app connects your food choices to your dopamine patterns. No platform links your screen behavior to your mental state and gives you a real alternative in real time.

UnPlug sits at the intersection of **mental wellness, behavioral science, nutrition, and AI** — and it is designed to work alongside the health ecosystem, not compete with it. The medical device founders building heart rate necklaces and glucose wearables are building the physical data layer. UnPlug is the behavioral intelligence that sits on top of it.
